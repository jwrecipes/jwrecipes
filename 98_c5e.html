SPICED CASHEWS

MAKES 400g

INGREDIENTS

400g cashew nuts

2 tsp olive or groundnut oil

2 tsp ground cumin

1½ tsp smoked paprika

METHOD

Preheat your oven to 190°C (fan 170°C, gas mark 5).

Mix all the ingredients together, then tip onto a baking tray and roast in the oven for 12–15 minutes until the nuts are crisp and lightly coloured. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with salt. These spiced nuts will keep in an airtight container for up to 5 days.
