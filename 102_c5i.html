SMOKED MACKEREL PÂTÉ

Raw cauliflower florets taste amazing with this pâté, which can be kept in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.

INGREDIENTS

300g smoked mackerel

75g crème fraiche

juice of 1 lemon

freshly ground black pepper

small bunch of chives, finely sliced

35g walnuts, roughly chopped

chopped carrots, cauliflower florets and sliced red pepper, to serve

METHOD

Remove the skin from the mackerel and, using your fingers, flake the fish into small pieces. Add the crème fraiche and lemon juice, along with a good grinding of black pepper. Use a fork to mix and mash until you’re happy with the consistency of your pâté – I like it with a bit of texture.

Stir in the chives, then top the pâté with the walnuts. Serve with the carrots, cauliflower and red pepper.
