BANANA AND BLUEBERRY OVERNIGHT OATS

This is a quick and easy breakfast to have ready to go, after jump-starting your day with a morning workout.

INGREDIENTS

1 banana, roughly chopped

75g full-fat yoghurt

250ml almond milk

1 scoop (30g) strawberry protein powder

100g rolled oats

handful of pistachios or other nuts, blueberries and raspberries to serve

METHOD

Place the banana, yoghurt, almond milk and protein powder into a blender and blend until smooth. Pour the mixture into a bowl and stir in the oats, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.

When ready to eat, top with the nuts, blueberries and raspberries.
