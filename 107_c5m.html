BEETROOT PROTEIN BROWNIES

I didn’t want to release a cookbook without a few sweet treats. These are yummy, and way healthier than your normal chocolate brownies. But they should still be enjoyed on occasion and not scoffed every day. A slice once a week, after a workout, is fine. These take more like 30 minutes than 15 minutes to make – but hey, you have to earn your treats!

MAKES ABOUT 16

LONGER RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

2 cooked and peeled beetroot (about 140g), roughly chopped

175g ground almonds

120g chestnut puree

30g cocoa powder

45g honey

1 scoop (30g) vanilla protein powder

2 tsp vanilla extract

4 eggs

METHOD

Preheat your oven to 180°C (fan 160°C, gas mark 4).

Place all the ingredients in a food processor and blitz to a smooth batter.

Tip the batter into a lined brownie tin (about 28cm x 15cm) and bake for 18 minutes.

Remove the brownie from the oven and leave to cool slightly before cutting into squares and chomping down.
