INGREDIENTS

200ml almond milk

1 granny smith apple, cored and roughly chopped

2 large handfuls (120g) of baby spinach leaves

1 scoop (30g) vanilla protein powder

75g rolled oats

METHOD

Place all the ingredients in a blender with a handful of ice and blitz until smooth.
