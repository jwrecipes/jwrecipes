MIDGET TREE AND PINE NUT PESTO

This is a great snack that will keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. Other green veg, such as kale and spinach, work well too. I like to eat this with chopped cauliflower, carrot and cucumber for dipping.

MAKE AHEAD

INGREDIENTS

2 heads of broccoli, broken into florets

4 tbsp pine nuts

3 tbsp finely grated parmesan

2 bunches of basil, leaves only

1 clove garlic, roughly chopped

juice and finely grated zest of 1 lemon

75ml olive oil

salt and pepper

chopped raw vegetables, to serve

METHOD

Bring a saucepan of water to the boil. Drop in the broccoli florets and cook for 1 minute. Drain in a sieve or colander, then rinse under cold running water.

Tip the broccoli into a blender and add the pine nuts, parmesan, basil, garlic, lemon juice and zest and olive oil. Season generously with salt and pepper, then pulse until virtually smooth â€“ you will most likely have to do quite a bit of pulsing and scraping.

Serve the pesto with chopped raw vegetables.
