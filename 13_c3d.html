GO-GO-GREEN SMOOTHIE

Mums always tell us to eat our greens, so here you go. If you’re not a big fan of leafy green veg, then this is the perfect opportunity for you to smash them in. Wheatgrass is very good for you – but, just like Marmite, it’s a love or hate kind of thing. If you don’t enjoy it, just leave it out and add more spinach or some kale instead.

INGREDIENTS

175ml coconut water

2 tbsp almond butter

25g fresh wheatgrass (or 5g powdered)

1 scoop (30g) vanilla protein powder

1 apple, cored and roughly chopped

20g flaxseeds

handful of baby spinach leaves

handful of ice cubes

METHOD

Put all the ingredients in a blender and blend on high for 1 minute or until the smoothie has reached your desired texture.
