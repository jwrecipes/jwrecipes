CHEAT’S BANANA AND ALMOND ICE CREAM

I love ice cream, so I thought I would share this healthier cheat’s ice cream recipe with you. Feel free to add other frozen fruit, such as strawberries or raspberries, to mix up the flavour.

LONGER RECIPE

(quick to make, but needs 4 hours in the freezer)

MAKE AHEAD

INGREDIENTS

4 bananas, peeled and cut into roughly similar-sized chunks

1 tbsp almond butter

50ml almond milk

1 scoop (30g) vanilla protein powder

toasted flaked almonds, to serve – optional

METHOD

Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper and spread the banana chunks over it in a single layer. Place the tray in your freezer and freeze for a minimum of 4 hours, or until the banana chunks are frozen solid.

Tip the frozen banana into a food processor, along with the almond butter, almond milk and the protein powder, then pulse until virtually smooth.

Serve your cheat’s ice cream topped with the toasted almonds, if using.
